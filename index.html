How it works
From estimate to earning — the four steps
1
Get your estimate here
Use the calculator above to understand your project's credit potential. Free, instant, no signup required.
2
Connect with a verified consultancy
We connect you with accredited project developers who handle your Project Design Document (PDD) and third-party validation.
3
Get registered and verified
Your project gets registered on Verra, Gold Standard, or India's CCTS registry. This typically takes 6–12 months end-to-end.
4
Earn recurring credit revenue
Credits are issued annually and sold to voluntary or compliance buyers. Revenue compounds as India's carbon market matures.
